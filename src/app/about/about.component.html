About

Vicara is an Immersive Technology company, pioneering in intuitive & immersive computing platforms. We develop technology & Solutions that augment the capability of humans to work with their digital surroundings. Vicara’s first product, the Kai, is a testament to this vision. The Kai is a gesture-based Natural User Interface, that allows you to communicate with your digital surroundings through simple gestures.

Founded in 2017, Vicara has grown to become a leader in immersive hardware. With a team of passionate engineers, researchers, designers & creators that are committed to building the future of extended reality, there’s no problem that Vicara can not solve.
